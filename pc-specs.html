My PC Specs & Full Setup

23 MAR 2026
3 MIN READ
BY STR1X3VO

People always ask what I'm running. Here's everything - full specs, monitors, peripherals, all with links so you can grab the same gear.

Core specs

CPU AMD Ryzen 5 5600X AMAZON
6 cores / 12 threads - 3.7GHz base, 4.6GHz boost. Handles everything I throw at it without breaking a sweat.
GPU Gigabyte RTX 3050 8GB AMAZON
Solid 1080p card. Runs Assetto Corsa with Pure and CSP beautifully and handles iRacing without issue.
Motherboard MSI B550 Gaming Gen3 AMAZON
ATX, AM4 socket. Ryzen 5000 ready, solid VRMs, no complaints.
Memory Crucial Pro 32GB Kit (2x16GB) DDR4 3200MHz
Dual channel, low latency. More than enough for gaming and having the VM running in the background at the same time.
Storage 500GB Crucial NVMe SSD (OS + games)
1TB SSD (extra games + files)
480GB SanDisk SSD (overflow)
Three drives because one was never enough.

Monitors

Running dual BenQ GW2790 monitors - both 27 inch, 1080p, 100Hz IPS panels. Great for sim racing since you get a wide field of view across two screens. Eye care tech is a bonus for long sessions.

Display x2 BenQ GW2790 27" 100Hz IPS AMAZON
1920x1080, 100Hz, IPS panel, low blue light, slim bezel. Clean looking dual monitor setup.

Audio

Audio SteelSeries Sonar (virtual audio device)
Software-based audio mixing. Lets you route game audio, Discord and mic separately - great for streaming and recording.
Headset HyperX Cloud 3 AMAZON
Comfortable over-ear gaming headset. Clear audio for sim racing and comms - solid build quality.
Microphone NJSJ USB Condenser Studio Microphone AMAZON
USB plug and play, RGB lighting, touch mute button, gain knob and headphone monitoring jack. Great budget mic for streaming and recording.

Sim Racing

Wheel Thrustmaster TMX Pro AMAZON
Force feedback racing wheel for Xbox and PC. Great entry level sim wheel - solid for Assetto Corsa and iRacing at 1080p.
Pedals Thrustmaster T3PA Add-On
3 pedal add-on - gas, brake and clutch. Wide adjustable pedal spacing and conical brake mod for more realistic feel. Compatible with the TMX Pro.

Games I run on this

This rig is built around Assetto Corsa with Pure by Peter Boese and CSP Preview 3.10 - running at max settings, looking stunning on both monitors.

Also running iRacing for competitive sim racing, Euro Truck Simulator 2 for a more relaxed drive, Fallout 76 for open world grinding, Rainbow Six Siege for competitive FPS, and Battlefield 6 for large scale combat. The 5600X and RTX 3050 handle all of these at 1080p without breaking a sweat.

// Worth upgrading If I was building fresh today I'd probably go RTX 4060 over the 3050 for a bit more headroom. But for 1080p sim racing this setup is more than capable and cost-effective.
